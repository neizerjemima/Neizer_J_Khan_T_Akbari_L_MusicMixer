    Welcome to Singing Tree, a Drag n' Drop Mixer!
    HOW TO PLAY

    • 1. Scroll through the Critters on the right and drop them into the dotted zones
    • 2. Test them out by clicking the play button,
        (you can also test their sounds by clicking the 'SOUNDS' tab above).
    • 3. Place as many characters into the treehouse and pond as yoy like!
        Mix & Match or Play them all at once!
